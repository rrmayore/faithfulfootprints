Our Family Story

Welcome to our family's journey of faith, love, and adventure. We are the Mayore family - devoted to each other, our faith, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

Our Love Story

Richard and Betty's journey began when they met through their church community. Their shared faith and values created an unbreakable bond that blossomed into love.

On May 2, 1999, they exchanged vows and embarked on a beautiful journey of marriage, committing to build a family rooted in their Seventh-day Adventist faith.

Over the years, God blessed them with three wonderful children - Shirley, Sika, and their youngest daughter. Together, they've built a life centered around faith, family values, and the joy of experiencing God's creation through their adventures.
